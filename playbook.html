Who This Is For

Written for the people navigating this transition

Sustainability Officers

Reporting demands are multiplying — GRESB, UK SRS, lender KPIs, investor requests. This playbook provides a framework for getting ahead of them, not just surviving each deadline.

Asset Managers

Sustainability data now affects acquisition diligence, hold-period monitoring, and exit pricing. Understand what good ESG data infrastructure looks like before its absence costs you.

Property Consultants

Your clients are asking for ESG help. The firms that build credible sustainability data capability now will capture a growing share of advisory spend.

Valuers

RICS 4th Edition makes ESG assessment mandatory from April 2026. This playbook covers what you need — the evidence, the data sources, and the professional opportunity.
